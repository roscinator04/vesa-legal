Common topics

Managing your subscription: subscriptions are handled through your Apple ID. To view, change, or cancel your subscription, open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then tap Subscriptions.

Restoring a purchase: if you've subscribed before and don't see your access, use the "Restore Purchases" option in the app.

Account and data: you can manage or delete your account from within the app's settings.
